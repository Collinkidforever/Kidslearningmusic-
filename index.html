Introduction

At Kids Learning Music, we believe that every child has the potential to become a great musician. Our mission is to provide fun and engaging music lessons to help children discover their love for music.

Our Story

My name's Colin Young, and I learned to play the piano from my grandpa, Bradley Woodruff Young. He taught me how to play Boogie Woogie and a few other songs. Then I learned from Arthur Meliazza, and I also taught myself, playing by ear. Eventually, I started making beats on my MacBook Pro using Logic Pro X. I recorded my voice, made beats, and created music that way. This journey inspired me to create Kids Learning Music, where I can share my passion and help children find their own musical paths.

Benefits of Learning Music

  • Enhances cognitive abilities
  • Boosts creativity and imagination
  • Improves coordination and motor skills
  • Encourages discipline and patience
  • Builds confidence and self-esteem
